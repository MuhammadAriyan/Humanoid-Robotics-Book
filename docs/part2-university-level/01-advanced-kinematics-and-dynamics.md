# Advanced Kinematics and Dynamics

## Learning Objectives

- Advanced kinematics aur dynamics ke asoolon ko samajhna.
- Robot ki harkat aur quwaton (forces) ke darmiyan taalluq ka mutala karna.

## Core Concepts

Advanced kinematics mein robot ke joints aur links ke darmiyan complex geometric taalluqat shamil hain, jismein forward aur inverse kinematics ka tafseeli mutala hota hai. Dynamics robot ki harkat ko paida karne wali quwaton aur unke asraat ka jaiza leta hai, jismein Newton-Euler ya Lagrange formulations istemal hote hain.

## Chapter Summary

Advanced kinematics aur dynamics robots ki harkat ko tafseeli tor par samajhne ke liye zaroori hain. Yeh engineers ko robots ko control karne aur unki harkat ko optimize karne mein madad dete hain.

## Glossary Terms

- **Forward Kinematics**: Joints ke angles se end-effector ki position aur orientation ka taayun karna.
- **Inverse Kinematics**: End-effector ki desired position aur orientation se joints ke angles ka taayun karna.
- **Dynamics**: Robot ki harkat ko paida karne wali quwaton aur unke asraat ka mutala.
