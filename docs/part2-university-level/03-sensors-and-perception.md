# Sensors and Perception

## Learning Objectives

- Mukhtalif qism ke sensors aur unke kaam ko samajhna.
- Robot mein mahol ko samajhne (perception) ke amal ka mutala karna.

## Core Concepts

Sensors robots ko apne mahol se maloomat hasil karne mein madad dete hain. Is mein vision sensors (cameras), range sensors (LiDAR, ultrasound), aur proprioceptive sensors (encoders) shamil hain. Perception robots ko in sensor data ko process kar ke mahol ki samajh paida karne ki ijazat deta hai.

## Chapter Summary

Sensors aur perception robotics ke liye bunyadi hain taake robots apne mahol mein behtar tareeqe se kaam kar saken. Mukhtalif sensors aur advanced perception techniques ke zariye robots mazeed zaheen aur khud mukhtar ban sakte hain.

## Glossary Terms

- **Vision Sensor**: Ek sensor (jese camera) jo tasveerain leta hai taake robot mahol ko dekh sake.
- **LiDAR**: Light Detection and Ranging, ek sensor jo laser ki madad se fasla napta hai aur 3D maps banata hai.
- **Proprioceptive Sensor**: Ek sensor jo robot ke jism ki apni harkat aur position ke bare mein maloomat deta hai.
