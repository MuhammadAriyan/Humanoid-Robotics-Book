# Actuators and Grippers

## Learning Objectives

- Mukhtalif qism ke actuators aur unke istemalat ko samajhna.
- Robot grippers ke design aur functions ka mutala karna.

## Core Concepts

Actuators wo devices hain jo robot mein harkat paida karte hain, jismein electric motors, hydraulic cylinders, aur pneumatic actuators shamil hain. Grippers wo end-effectors hain jo cheezon ko pakarne aur manipulate karne ke liye istemal hote hain, jismein jaw grippers, vacuum grippers, aur soft grippers shamil hain.

## Chapter Summary

Actuators aur grippers robots ko physical mahol mein harkat karne aur cheezon ko manipulate karne ki ijazat dete hain. Inka sahi intikhab aur design robot ki performance ke liye intehai ahem hai.

## Glossary Terms

- **Hydraulic Actuator**: Ek actuator jo dabe hue paani ya tel ka istemal karta hai taake quwat paida kar sake.
- **Pneumatic Actuator**: Ek actuator jo dabe hue hawa ka istemal karta hai taake harkat paida kar sake.
- **Gripper**: Robot ka end-effector jo cheezon ko pakarne aur uthane ke liye istemal hota hai.
