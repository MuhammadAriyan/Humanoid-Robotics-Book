# Introduction to Robotics

## Learning Objectives

- Robotics ki bunyadi tareef aur iska maqsad samajhna.
- Robotics ki mukhtalif qismen aur unke istemalat ka jaiza lena.

## Core Concepts

Robotics science, engineering, aur technology ka ek shoba hai jo robots ke design, tameer, operation, aur istemal se mutaliq hai. Robots ka maqsad insani koshishon ko asan banana aur khatarnak maholon mein kaam karna hai.

## Glossary Terms

- **Robot**: Ek machine jo insani dakhaliyat ke baghair ya kam dakhaliyat ke saath, khud mukhtar tareeqe se kaam kar sakti hai.
- **Robotics**: Robots ke design, tameer, operation, aur istemal ka mutala.
