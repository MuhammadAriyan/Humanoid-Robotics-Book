# Introduction to Robot Movement

## Learning Objectives

- Robot ki harkat ke bunyadi usoolon ko samajhna.
- Degrees of freedom (DOF) aur kinematics ke tasawwur se waqfiyat hasil karna.

## Core Concepts

Robots ki harkat ko samajhna robotics ka bunyadi juzv hai. Is mein degrees of freedom (DOF) shamil hain, jo robot ke jism ke mukhtalif hisson ki azadana harkaton ki tadaad batate hain. Kinematics robot ki harkat ki geometry ka mutala karta hai, baghair quwaton (forces) ko mad-de-nazar rakhe.

## Chapter Summary

Robot ki harkat ko degrees of freedom aur kinematics ke zariye samjha ja sakta hai. Yeh robot ko mukhtalif tareeqon se harkat karne aur apne mahol mein kaam karne ke qabil banata hai.

## Glossary Terms

- **Degrees of Freedom (DOF)**: Robot ke jism ke mukhtalif hisson ki azadana harkaton ki tadaad.
- **Kinematics**: Robot ki harkat ki geometry ka mutala, baghair quwaton ko mad-de-nazar rakhe.
