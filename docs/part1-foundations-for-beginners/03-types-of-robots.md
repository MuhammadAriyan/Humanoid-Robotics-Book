# Types of Robots

## Learning Objectives

- Robots ki mukhtalif aqsaam ko pehchanna.
- Har qism ke robot ki khasusiyat aur istemalat ko samajhna.

## Core Concepts

Robots ko mukhtalif tareeqon se taqseem kiya ja sakta hai, jismein unke maqsad, harkat ki salahiyat, aur mahol ke saath interaction shamil hai. Aam aqsaam mein industrial robots, mobile robots, aur humanoid robots shamil hain.

## Chapter Summary

Robots ki mukhtalif aqsaam maujood hain, jo har aik khaas maqsad aur mahol ke liye design kiye gaye hain. Unki aqsaam unki shakal, harkat, aur zehni salahiyaton par munhasir hain.

## Glossary Terms

- **Mobile Robot**: Ek robot jo apni harkat ke liye pahiyon, tracks, ya pairon ka istemal karta hai, aur mukhtalif maholon mein safar kar sakta hai.
- **Humanoid Robot**: Ek robot jo insani shakal-o-surat aur harkat ki naqal karta hai.
