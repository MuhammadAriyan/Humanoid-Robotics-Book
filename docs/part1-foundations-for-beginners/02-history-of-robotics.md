# History of Robotics

## Learning Objectives

- Robotics ki tareekhi irtaqa ko samajhna.

## Core Concepts

Robotics ki tareekh qadeem tehzeebon se shuru hoti hai jahan khudkaar machines ka tasawwur maujood tha. Modern robotics 20veen sadi mein tezi se phaili hai, jismein industrial robots aur AI-powered systems shamil hain.

## Chapter Summary

Robotics ne sadiyon ka safar tai kiya hai, qadeem tasawwurat se le kar aaj ke mutharrak aur zaheen machines tak. Iski tareekh insani aqal aur technology ki taraqqi ka saboot hai.

## Glossary Terms

- **Automatons**: Khudkaar machines ya khilone jo insani ya janwaron ki naqal karte the, aksar qadeem zamanon mein banaye jate the.
- **Industrial Robot**: Ek robot jo sanati paimane par istemal hota hai, jese welding, painting, ya assembly ke liye.
