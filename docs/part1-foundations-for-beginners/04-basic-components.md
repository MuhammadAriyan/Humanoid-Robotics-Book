# Basic Components

## Learning Objectives

- Robots ke bunyadi ajzaa (components) ko pehchanna.
- Sensors, actuators, aur controllers ke bunyadi kaam ko samajhna.

## Core Concepts

Har robot mukhtalif ajzaa se mil kar banta hai jo usay mahol ke saath interaction aur harkat karne ke qabil banate hain. Bunyadi ajzaa mein sensors (mahol ko mehsoos karne ke liye), actuators (harkat paida karne ke liye), aur controllers (robot ke dimagh ke tor par kaam karne ke liye) shamil hain.

## Chapter Summary

Sensors, actuators, aur controllers robots ke bunyadi ajzaa hain. Inki samajh robotics ke mukhtalif pahluon ko samajhne ke liye zaroori hai.

## Glossary Terms

- **Sensor**: Ek device jo mahol se data ikattha karta hai, jese light, awaz, ya fasla.
- **Actuator**: Ek device jo electric energy ko physical harkat mein tabdeel karta hai, jese motor.
- **Controller**: Robot ka hissa jo sensors se data process karta hai aur actuators ko harkat ke liye hidayat deta hai.
